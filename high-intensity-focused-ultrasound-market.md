<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) market is segmented into two primary types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided systems. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target tissues, often favored for its accessibility and efficiency in procedures like tumor ablation. Conversely, MR-guided HIFU leverages magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced visualization and treatment planning, providing superior precision and safety in complex cases. Both types are integral in non-invasive therapies across oncology and other medical fields, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
