<p><p>Industrial robotics in the electronic and electrical market encompasses various robot types, each suited for specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotating joints for complex tasks, while Cartesian robots use linear movements along three axes for precise operations. SCARA robots offer flexibility and speed for assembly tasks, cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear motions, and parallel robots excel in precise positioning for assembly or pick-and-place tasks. Other types include delta robots and collaborative robots, enhancing efficiency and versatility in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
