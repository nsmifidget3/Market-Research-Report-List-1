<p><p>The Diamond End Mills market is segmented into three main types: Chemical Vapor Deposition Diamond (CVD), Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD), and Amorphous Diamond. CVD diamonds are created through a vapor process, resulting in high-quality, uniform coatings suitable for precision machining. PCD consists of diamond particles sintered together, ideal for hard materials and high abrasion resistance. Amorphous Diamond features a non-crystalline form, providing excellent hardness and wear resistance, making it versatile in various industrial applications. Together, these types cater to diverse machining needs.</p></p>
