<p><p>The greenhouse heaters market comprises three primary types: electric heaters, gas heaters, and paraffin heaters. Electric heaters are popular for their efficiency and ease of use, providing consistent heat without emissions. Gas heaters are favored for their ability to generate significant warmth quickly, often used in larger greenhouses. Paraffin heaters offer a cost-effective alternative, utilizing paraffin oil to generate heat; however, they require proper ventilation. Each type serves distinct needs based on greenhouse size, heating requirement, and operational preferences.</p></p>
