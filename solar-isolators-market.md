<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar isolators market is primarily categorized into two types: 2 pole and 4 pole isolators. </p><p>2 pole isolators are designed for single-phase applications, commonly found in residential solar systems, allowing for effective disconnection from the grid. </p><p>In contrast, 4 pole isolators are used for three-phase applications, suitable for larger commercial installations, providing safe disconnection of all phases. Both types are essential for ensuring safe operation and maintenance of solar power systems, preventing electrical hazards.</p></p>
