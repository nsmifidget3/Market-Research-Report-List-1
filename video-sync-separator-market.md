<p><strong>The Video Sync Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite</li><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video sync separator market comprises three primary types: composite, horizontal, and vertical sync separators. Composite sync separators extract synchronization signals from composite video signals, enabling simplified processing. Horizontal sync separators focus on isolating horizontal sync pulses to maintain sequential frame timing, ensuring smooth video playback. Vertical sync separators are dedicated to capturing vertical sync pulses, crucial for frame stability in displays. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring accurate synchronization in various video applications, enhancing overall visual performance.</p></p>
