<p><ul><li>40MHz – 174MHz (VHF)</li><li>200MHz – 512MHz (UHF)</li><li>700MHz – 1000MHz (SHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Land Mobile Radio (LMR) market encompasses various frequency bands used for mobile communication. The VHF range (40MHz – 174MHz) is ideal for wide coverage and is commonly used by emergency services. The UHF band (200MHz – 512MHz) provides better penetration in urban areas and is favored for commercial and public safety communications. The SHF band (700MHz – 1000MHz) offers high data rates and is increasingly utilized for advanced applications, including broadband services and digital communications in critical sectors.</p></p>
