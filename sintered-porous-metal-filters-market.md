<p><p>Sintered porous metal filters are categorized based on their porosity levels, affecting their filtration capabilities and applications. Low porosity filters (below 30%) offer strong structural integrity and are ideal for coarse filtration. Medium porosity filters (30-60%) balance strength and filtration efficiency, suitable for various industrial applications. High porosity filters (above 60%) maximize flow rates and are commonly used for fine filtration, allowing for higher throughput while maintaining effective separation of particles. Each type serves specific needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
