<p><p>The throat cancer therapeutics market encompasses three primary treatment modalities: surgery, radiotherapy, and chemotherapy. Surgery involves the physical removal of tumors and affected tissues to achieve a cure. Radiotherapy employs high-energy radiation to target and destroy cancer cells, often used post-surgery to eliminate residual disease. Chemotherapy utilizes systemic drugs to target rapidly dividing cancer cells, either as a standalone treatment or in combination with surgery and radiotherapy to enhance effectiveness and manage advanced stages of the disease.</p></p>
