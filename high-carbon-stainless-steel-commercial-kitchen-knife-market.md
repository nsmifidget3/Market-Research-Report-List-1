<p><p>The High Carbon Stainless Steel Commercial Kitchen Knife market is divided into two main types: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade knives are manufactured by cutting a blade shape from a large sheet of steel, resulting in lighter, more flexible knives that are generally more affordable. In contrast, forged blade knives are created by heating and shaping a solid piece of steel, leading to stronger, heavier, and more durable knives, preferred by professional chefs for their balance and precision.</p></p>
