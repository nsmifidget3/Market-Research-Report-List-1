<p><p>The general purpose contactor market includes two primary types: DC contactors and AC contactors. DC contactors are designed for direct current applications, handling lower voltage and current levels typically found in battery-operated systems and electric vehicles. In contrast, AC contactors are used for alternating current applications, suitable for powering motors, lighting, and other industrial equipment. Both types play a crucial role in electrical control systems, enabling efficient power management and automation across diverse sectors, including manufacturing, automotive, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
