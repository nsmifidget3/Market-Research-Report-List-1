<p><p>The Driver Alert System market consists of three main types for enhancing driver awareness. Audible alarms provide auditory signals to alert drivers of potential drowsiness or distraction. Steering alerts use vibrations in the steering wheel to alert drivers without diverting their attention from the road, promoting safer driving habits. Seat vibration alerts engage the driver through tactile feedback, alerting them to potential hazards. Together, these systems aim to reduce accidents by maintaining driver focus and preventing fatigue-related incidents.</p></p>
