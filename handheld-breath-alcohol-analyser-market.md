<p><p>The handheld breath alcohol analyser market is categorized into two primary types: direct sample and passive sample. Direct sample analysers require individuals to actively blow into the device, allowing for precise measurement of alcohol content in breath. In contrast, passive sample analysers can detect alcohol levels in the environment or from a person without the need for a direct breath sample, often used in surveillance or public safety settings. Both types serve vital roles in law enforcement and personal safety.</p></p>
