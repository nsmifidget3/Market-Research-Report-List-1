<p><p>The oral OTC analgesics market encompasses three primary types: Acetaminophen, Salicylates, and Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs). Acetaminophen, commonly known as Tylenol, is utilized for pain relief and fever reduction without anti-inflammatory properties. Salicylates, including aspirin, provide pain relief and possess anti-inflammatory effects but may cause gastrointestinal issues. NSAIDs, such as ibuprofen and naproxen, are effective for pain, inflammation, and fever, commonly used for conditions like arthritis and headaches. Each type offers distinct benefits suited to various consumer needs.</p></p>
