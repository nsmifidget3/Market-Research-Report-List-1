<p><p>The Ultrasonic Surgical System market comprises various technologies catering to different medical needs. High-intensity Focused Ultrasonic (HIFU) Ablators concentrate ultrasound energy to ablate tissue non-invasively. Magnetic Resonance-guided Focused Ultrasonic (MRGFUS) Ablators utilize MRI guidance for precise targeting in therapeutic applications. Ultrasonic Surgical Ablation Systems employ ultrasound for tissue removal or modification in surgical procedures. Shock wave therapy Systems provide non-invasive treatment by delivering pressure waves to promote healing and reduce pain, enhancing patient outcomes across various medical fields.</p></p>
