Solvent-based acrylics involve organic solvents, providing excellent durability and finish, often preferred for industrial applications. Other types may include specialty formulations, such as 100% solids or high-performance coatings, catering to specific requirements in various sectors, including automotive, construction, and arts. Each type has distinct properties and applications.</p></p>
