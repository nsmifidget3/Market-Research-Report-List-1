<p><p>The liquid leak detection sensors market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves placing sensors directly into the liquid system, allowing for precise monitoring and immediate response to leaks. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection utilizes external sensors to identify leaks without direct contact with the liquid, often using acoustic, thermal, or electromagnetic methods. Both approaches aim to enhance safety, minimize damage, and reduce operational costs in various industries.</p></p>
