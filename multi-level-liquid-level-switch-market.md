<p><p>The multi-level liquid level switch market is categorized into two main types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type switches operate by directly interacting with the liquid, often using floats or conductive materials to detect levels. In contrast, non-contact type switches utilize technologies like ultrasonic or radar sensors to measure liquid levels without physical contact. Both types serve various industries, providing critical solutions for monitoring liquid levels in tanks and reservoirs, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
