<p><p>The prestressed concrete strand tensioning equipment market consists of two primary types: single strand tension and multi-strand tension. Single strand tensioning involves using one prestressing strand at a time, offering simplicity and precise control, making it ideal for smaller projects. In contrast, multi-strand tensioning utilizes several strands simultaneously, allowing for larger-scale applications and faster operation, increasing overall efficiency. Both methods play a vital role in enhancing the durability and load-bearing capacity of concrete structures.</p></p>
