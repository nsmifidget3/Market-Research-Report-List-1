<p><p>The hydraulic metering pump market consists of several types, including diaphragm hydraulic metering pumps, piston hydraulic metering pumps, and others. Diaphragm pumps use flexible membranes to accurately dispense fluids, ideal for handling corrosive substances. Piston pumps utilize a reciprocating piston mechanism, providing high precision and pressure capabilities, suitable for viscous fluids. Other types may include gear or peristaltic pumps, each chosen based on specific application needs in industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
