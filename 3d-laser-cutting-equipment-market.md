<p><p>The 3D laser cutting equipment market primarily includes fiber lasers and carbon dioxide (CO2) lasers. Fiber lasers utilize solid-state technology, offering high efficiency, faster cutting speeds, and precise accuracy, making them ideal for metals and materials with reflective surfaces. In contrast, CO2 lasers use gas to emit a beam, functioning effectively on non-metal materials like plastics and wood. Each type serves distinct applications, with fiber lasers excelling in metal fabrication and CO2 lasers catering to diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
