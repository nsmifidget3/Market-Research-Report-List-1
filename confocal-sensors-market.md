<p><p>Confocal sensors are advanced optical devices used for high-resolution measurement and imaging. The monochromatic confocal sensors primarily utilize a single wavelength of light, offering precise depth resolution and enhanced sensitivity, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate surface profiling. In contrast, polychromatic confocal sensors employ multiple wavelengths, allowing for diverse material characterization and improved depth analysis. Together, these technologies cater to various industries, including manufacturing, biomedical, and semiconductor, by ensuring accurate dimensional and structural assessments.</p></p>
