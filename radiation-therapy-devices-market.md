<p><p>The radiation therapy devices market encompasses various techniques aimed at treating cancer. External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT) delivers targeted radiation from outside the body, utilizing advanced technologies like linear accelerators. Internal Beam Radiation Therapy, or brachytherapy, involves placing radioactive sources inside or near tumors for localized treatment. Stereotactic Technology offers precise, high-dose radiation delivery with minimal exposure to surrounding tissues, allowing for effective treatment of small tumors. Together, these methods enhance treatment efficacy and patient outcomes in oncology care.</p></p>
