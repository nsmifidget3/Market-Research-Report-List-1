<p><ul><li>Long Arc Lamp</li><li>Short Arc Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arc lamp market is categorized into two main types: long arc lamps and short arc lamps. Long arc lamps feature a greater distance between electrodes, producing a steady light source ideal for applications like floodlights and large-scale projections. In contrast, short arc lamps have closely spaced electrodes, generating intense illumination suitable for high-precision tasks such as film projection and industrial uses. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to different lighting requirements in various industries, including entertainment, manufacturing, and medical applications.</p></p>
