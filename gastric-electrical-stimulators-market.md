<p><p>The gastric electrical stimulators market comprises two primary types: high-frequency gastric electrical stimulators and low-frequency gastric electrical stimulators. High-frequency stimulators operate at greater pulse rates, aimed at enhancing gastric motility and reducing symptoms of nausea and vomiting, often used in cases of gastroparesis. In contrast, low-frequency stimulators work at lower pulse rates, primarily focusing on stimulating gastric contractions to aid digestion. Both types cater to patients with gastrointestinal disorders, improving their quality of life by managing symptoms effectively.</p></p>
