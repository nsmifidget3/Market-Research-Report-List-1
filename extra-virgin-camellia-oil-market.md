<p><strong>The Extra Virgin Camellia Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expelling</li><li>Lixiviation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extra virgin camellia oil is primarily produced through two market types: expelling and lixiviation. The expelling process involves mechanically pressing the seeds to extract oil, retaining its natural flavors and nutrients, often preferred for its purity. In contrast, the lixiviation process employs a solvent to dissolve the oil, followed by separation, which can yield higher quantities but may compromise some quality. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences, with expelling appealing to those seeking natural products and lixiviation meeting larger-scale demands.</p></p>
