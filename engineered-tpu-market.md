<p><p>The engineered TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) market is categorized into two primary types: foam and elastomer. TPU foams are lightweight, flexible materials often used in cushioning applications, providing excellent impact resistance and vibration dampening. Conversely, TPU elastomers are known for their superior durability and flexibility, making them suitable for demanding applications like automotive parts, seals, and footwear. Both types leverage the unique properties of TPU, such as abrasion resistance and elasticity, to meet diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
