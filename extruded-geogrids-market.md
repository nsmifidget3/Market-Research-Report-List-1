<p><p>The extruded geogrids market consists of two main types: uniaxial and biaxial geogrids. Uniaxial geogrids are designed to provide strength and stability in one direction, commonly used for soil reinforcement in retaining walls and slopes. In contrast, biaxial geogrids offer multidirectional support, enhancing load distribution and overall soil stability in applications like pavement and subgrade reinforcement. Both types are essential for improving structural integrity and performance in a variety of construction and civil engineering projects.</p></p>
