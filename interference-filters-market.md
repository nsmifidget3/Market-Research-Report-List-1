<p><ul><li>High-Pass Type</li><li>Low-Pass Type</li><li>Bandpass Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interference filters are optical devices used to selectively transmit or block specific wavelength ranges of light. The high-pass type allows wavelengths above a certain cutoff to pass while blocking shorter wavelengths. Conversely, low-pass filters permit shorter wavelengths to pass while attenuating longer ones. Bandpass filters combine these properties, transmitting a specific range of wavelengths while blocking those outside this range. Each type serves various applications in fields like telecommunications, imaging, and scientific research, catering to specific light filtering requirements.</p></p>
