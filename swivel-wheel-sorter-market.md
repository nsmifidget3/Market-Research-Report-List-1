<p><p>Swivel wheel sorters are categorized based on their steering angles: 30°, 45°, and 90°. The 30° steering type offers enhanced maneuverability for tight spaces, making it suitable for small-scale operations. The 45° steering facilitates a balanced approach, optimizing both agility and speed, ideal for medium-sized sorting tasks. The 90° steering, while less agile, excels in stability and direct sorting efficiency, making it effective for larger applications requiring straight and robust movement paths. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
